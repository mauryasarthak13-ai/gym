Healthy Weight Gain Tips 💪

1. Eat More Calories

  • Eat more than your body burns.
  • Have 5–6 meals a day instead of 3.

2. Choose Nutrient-Rich Foods

  • Carbs: Rice, roti, potatoes, oats
  • Proteins: Milk, eggs, paneer, dal, chicken
  • Healthy fats: Nuts, peanut butter, ghee

3. Drink High-Calorie Beverages

  • Milkshakes like banana or mango shake
  • Smoothies with milk and dry fruits

4. Do Strength Training

  • Push-ups, squats, and weight lifting
  • Helps build muscle instead of fat

5. Eat Energy-Dense Snacks

  • Banana with peanut butter
  • Dry fruits like almonds, cashews, raisins
  • Cheese sandwiches

6. Don’t Skip Meals

  • Eat regularly even if you are not hungry

7. Get Proper Sleep

  • Sleep 7–9 hours daily
  • Helps muscle growth

8. Avoid Junk-Only Diet

  • Do not rely only on fast food
  • Focus on healthy meals
Bonus Tip: Track your weight weekly and stay patient. Healthy weight gain takes time!
